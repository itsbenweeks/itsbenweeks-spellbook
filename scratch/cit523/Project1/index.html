Background:

An international small business owner approached you to write a program.

The company purchases specific products every month and ships internationally every month.

They need to know what the estimated profit would be based these constant product shipments.

Product Requirements:

The company purchases and ships these products in monthly bases.

Product List:                                Quantity

Blush kit for $29.99                         1

Love Passion Lotion for $9.00                1

Love Spell Gift Kit for $22.00                1

Gift box for $0.50                        1

Gift bag for $0.25                        1

State Sales tax:

The business keeps their purchase contacts a super secret, however they

could be purchasing these products from one of these States.

Massachusetts

Alabama

California

Shipping:

Shipping company charges 20% of the total cost of the product.

Doesn't matter which state

Tariff:

The country in question has a 30% tariff

Exchange Rate:

The country in which the company exports to has an exchange rate of 1.79.

Profit Margin:

The company target for profit is 300%.

Screen Display:

The owner wants to see a summary of one month.

DELIVERABLES:

A python script that executes the program requirements
